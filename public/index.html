Chicago Urban Tree Dispatch — how to read it
  1. 1
    The map shows urgency. Each neighborhood is shaded by how much tree-service pressure it's under — red = critical down to green = clear. Zoom in to reveal individual open 311 request pins.
  2. 2
    The queue ranks where to send crews. Sort by Urgency, Neglect (longest since service), or Trending (fastest-rising). Use search to jump to any neighborhood.
  3. 3
    Click a zone for the field brief. An AI dispatch summary, complaint trend, repeat-problem addresses, service history, and live canopy data from OpenStreetMap & iNaturalist.
What is CSI?
Canopy Stress Index (0–100) blends five signals into one dispatch priority: open & unresolved requests, complaint surge vs. last month, drought stress, time since last service, and community vulnerability (Chicago Hardship Index). Higher = more urgent. Flip the map to Social vulnerability to see where the most at-risk communities are.
